Needs to be reliable and cheap

XLR:
- Locks really nicely
- Readily available
- Has 3 pins which should be enough, plus 4 pin variant isn't too uncommon
- Kinda expensive though

USB-C:
- Means we can expand stuff to have each buzzer be its own HID device for buzzin.live etc?
- Cheap and readily available
- Doesn't lock
- Would require each buzzer to have a relatively expensive [[Microcontroller]]

JST:
- Cheap and secure
- Hard to replace, not exactly hot-swappable
- Might work with daisy-chain buzzers if we go for a DIP switch design though

RJ45:
- Cheap
- Locks but not particularly well
- More than fast enough, really easily accessible
